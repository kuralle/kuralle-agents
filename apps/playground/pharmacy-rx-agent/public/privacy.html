💊 Pharmacy Rx Agent — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026
This is a demonstration service. Pharmacy Rx Agent is a technology demo of a conversational ordering assistant. It is not a licensed pharmacy, dispenses no medication, and should not be used with real prescriptions or real personal health information. Please do not send sensitive medical data you would not want processed by third-party AI services.

This policy explains what information the Pharmacy Rx Agent ("the Service", "we") collects when you interact with it over WhatsApp or the web chat, how that information is used, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Messages you send — the text, selections, and any images (e.g. a prescription photo) you submit to the assistant.
  • Your WhatsApp identifier — the phone number (WhatsApp ID) that messages you, used to route the conversation and your cart back to you.
  • Conversation state — your current cart, order status, and chat history for the thread, so the assistant can continue a conversation.

We do not collect payment card details. Payment links in the demo are simulated and take no real money.

How we use it

  • To read your request (including understanding a prescription image), check demo inventory, manage a cart, and respond to you.
  • To maintain conversation continuity within your thread.

We do not sell your information or use it for advertising.

Third-party processors

To operate, your messages are processed by the following providers:

ProviderPurpose
Meta / WhatsAppDelivers messages to and from the WhatsApp number.
OpenAIAI model that interprets your messages and images and generates replies.
CloudflareHosts the service and stores conversation/cart state.

Data retention & deletion

Conversation and cart state is stored only to provide the Service and may be cleared at any time. As this is a demo, data may be deleted without notice. To request deletion of your conversation data, contact us using the details below or send delete my data in the chat.

Your choices

  • Stop using the Service at any time; you can also block the WhatsApp number.
  • Request access to, or deletion of, the data associated with your phone number.

Children

The Service is not directed to children and should not be used by anyone under 18.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
