# Acme Corp Policies

## Refund Policy

Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, a partial refund of 50% is available up to 60 days. No refunds are issued after 60 days.

Digital products are non-refundable once the download link has been accessed. Physical products must be returned in original packaging.

## EU Refund Policy

Under EU consumer protection law, customers in the European Union are entitled to a 14-day cooling-off period for online purchases. This applies regardless of reason and the customer does not need to provide justification.

For EU customers, return shipping costs are covered by Acme Corp for the first return. Subsequent returns within the same quarter will have shipping deducted from the refund.

## Shipping Policy

Standard shipping: 5-7 business days, free for orders over $50.
Express shipping: 1-2 business days, $12.99 flat rate.
International shipping: 10-21 business days, rates vary by destination.

All orders include tracking. Signature is required for orders over $200.

## Privacy Policy

Acme Corp collects only the data necessary to process orders and provide customer support. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Data retention: Order history is retained for 7 years for tax compliance. Customer accounts can be deleted upon request, with a 30-day grace period.

GDPR: EU customers can request a full data export or deletion at any time via support@acme.com.

## Warranty Policy

All physical products come with a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, or unauthorized modifications.

Extended warranty: Available for purchase within 30 days of the original order. Extends coverage to 3 years.

## Account Policy

Accounts are free to create. A valid email address is required for verification. Accounts inactive for 2 years may be flagged for deletion with 60 days notice.

Two-factor authentication is available and recommended for all accounts. Acme Corp will never ask for your password via email or phone.

## Billing Policy

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Invoices are generated automatically and sent to the registered email.

Disputed charges should be reported within 30 days. Acme Corp will investigate and respond within 5 business days.

Subscription billing occurs on the same day each month. Failed payments are retried 3 times over 7 days before the subscription is suspended.
